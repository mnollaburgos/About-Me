About Me

I am an aspiring full-stack developer. I currently reside in Orlando, Fl, though I am originally from San Juan, PR. I decided to pursue coding in late October of 2020. I had always been vastly interested in Web Development as well as I was challenged by the COVID-19 Pandemic to find a more secure form of employment. Thus far I have achieved an acute understanding of HTML and CSS. At the moment, I find myself fighting through the trenches of JavaScript. I am thrilled for the adventure and I look forward to many more. I love to talk about myself when I have nothing else to say.
